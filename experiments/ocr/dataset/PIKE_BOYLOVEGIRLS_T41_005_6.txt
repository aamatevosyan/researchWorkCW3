AFTER THAT NIGHT, TONY SEEMED TO VANISH FROM THE FACE OF THE EARTH. THAT FALL, I TOOK A JOB AS A SECRETARY, AND BART WENT OFF TO COLLEGE. HE WROTE OFTEN, AND I SAW HIM DURING VACA- TIONS AND ON OCCA- SIONAL WEEKENDS, WHEN HE COULD COME DOWN. A YEAR PASSED, AND THEN PART OF ANOTHER.. AND THEN ONE NIGHT.
